I'm a Master's student in the Department of Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon University, and a Visiting Research Assistant in the AI Lab at Stanford with Prof. Emma Brunskill.

In the past, I spent 5 amazing years at the Indian Institute of Technology - Delhi (IITD) where I majored in EECS. At IITD I got interested in AI research while working with Prof. Mausam. I also great enjoyed working with Prof. Aditya Parameswaran and Prof. Hari Sundaram at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

My focus is on building autonomous agents that learn interpretable behaviors through efficient interaction with the world. I think about these questions mainly through the lens of Reinforcement Learning, but I'm always looking for inspiration from other areas of Artificial Intelligence. Another area I'm passionate about is applying AI to solve real-world problems, and I work on building algorithms and systems for improving intelligent tutoring systems.
