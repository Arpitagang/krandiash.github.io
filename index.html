I'm currently a Master's student in the Department of Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon University, and a Research Assistant in the School of Computer Science with Prof. Emma Brunskill. In the past, I spent 5 amazing years in the Indian Institute of Technology at Delhi (IIT-D) where I majored in Electrical Engineering. At IIT-D I was supervised by Prof. Mausam, with whom I worked on creating an end-to-end solution for managing tasks on micro-task crowdsourcing platforms. I also had the opportunity to work with Prof. Aditya Parameswaran and Prof. Hari Sundaram at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. We developed crowd-powered algorithms for clustering complicated data, as well as for visual recognition tasks.

I'm currently exploring problems in Reinforcement Learning (RL) as well as thinking about how we can apply RL and Machine Learning to improve Intelligent Tutoring Systems. I'm also really interested in engendering progress in AI that is inclusive in terms of its economic effects, as well as minimizing negative impact from automation.
