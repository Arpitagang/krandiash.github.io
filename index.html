Karan Goel

I am a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at Rutgers University working under the supervision of Prof. Waheed Bajwa. My research interests lie in the areas of distributed optimization and information processing.

Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant from 2016-17 and also graduated with a Master's degree from Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi in 2016. I worked in audio signal processing, specifically on source separation problem.

If you'd like to collaborate on research, chat with me about AI or network with me then feel free to drop me a line! I'm also happy to give free advice if that's something you're interested in!
